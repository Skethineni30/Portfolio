Scratch Project

This project was done in CSP and was finished in September of 2019

In the scratch project, me and Amrith KrishnaKumar created a player based story on a robber named Bob. The robber aimed to steal from a library. The player had to make the moves of moving left, or right. If he moved left, he would get a guitar. If he moved right. He would get the baseball. A cop comes and you must run away. You must escape, or else you will lose. Play to find out how Bob will run away from the cop.

Click the image to play the game


After each pause in dialogue, press the right or left arrow to move around and create your story.


MIT App Inventor Project

This project was done in CSP during September and October of 2019

In my app inventor project, Aadi Sharma and I created a game called Collect the Gold. The game was based on Get the Gold, a game given my MIT app inventor. In the game, ou use the wasd keys and move around as a pirate ship. Coins spawn all around the map and you must collect them. The coins change locations every couple seconds. There is an enemy in the middle and if you touch it, you lose. The enemy also moves around every couple seconds. Collect all the coins to win.
